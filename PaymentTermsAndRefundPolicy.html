Payment Terms and Refund Policy

1. Payment Terms

  • Full payment for participation in a program is required at the time of registration unless otherwise agreed upon in writing by Ampex Education.
  • Programs that include a trial window will grant access to the trial period only after full payment has been made.

2. Refund Policy During Trial Window

  • Students may request a refund of the program fees paid within 2 days of registration if they are within the trial window.
  • Refund requests during the trial window will be reviewed by Ampex Education and are subject to approval based on our internal policies.
  • Once the trial window has closed, no refunds will be allowed for any reason whatsoever.
  • Regardless of attendance or participation in the program after the trial window, refunds will not be granted.
